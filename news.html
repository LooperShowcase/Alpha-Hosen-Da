Portuguese soccer star Cristiano Ronaldo performed a traditional sword dance on Saudi Arabia’s foundation day. Saudi Football Club Al Nasr and Star Footballer have shared a video on their Twitter accounts on Saudi Arabia’s Foundation Day, in which several players, including Cristiano Ronaldo, are seen celebrating on the Foundation Day. In the video released on Twitter, it can be seen that the Portuguese star and five-time World Player of the Year award, Cristiano Ronaldo, along with other players of Al-Nasr Club, celebrated the establishment day in Saudi clothing. Cristiano Ronaldo also participated in the traditional Saudi sword dance ‘Al Arada’. In the video, the Portuguese star can be seen drinking Saudi coffee, dancing to popular songs. Ronaldo shared the video on Instagram in which he wrote, ‘Happy Foundation Day to Saudi Arabia’. He said that the experience of participating in the celebration of the foundation day of Saudi Arabia was wonderful. Saudi Arabia celebrates its foundation day on February 22, which is actually a day to commemorate the establishment of the Saudi state 300 years ago, whose formal establishment was announced by Imam Muhammad bin Saud in 1727.
